## Saguenay's Outdoor Workout Lowdown: February 28, 2026

Alright, let's cut to the chase. The calendar says late February, and Saguenay is playing its part. While the official temperature is a crisp -6.6°C, that's just a number. The *real* story today is the wind. Forecasts are calling for strong south-westerly winds, gusting anywhere from 32 to a brutal 70 km/h. This isn't just a breezy day; this is "feel it in your bones" cold, dropping the wind chill into the -13°C to -15°C range. If you're susceptible to respiratory irritation in the cold, consider scaling back your intensity or moving your workout indoors.

Combined with those strong winds, visibility on open paths could be reduced, and conditions underfoot will be slippery. So, watch your step unless you enjoy impromptu ice-skating demonstrations.
