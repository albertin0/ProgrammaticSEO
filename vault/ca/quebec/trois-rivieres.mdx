## Black Ice: The Silent Winter Predator
Yes, it's clear out, but the ground is frozen solid. With temperatures dipping well below freezing, any melt from yesterday or even a tiny bit of humidity can turn into invisible black ice overnight. This is especially true in shaded spots, underpasses, or less-trafficked paths like those in the **Parc Linéaire des Forges**. A fall on ice can mean a twisted ankle, a broken wrist, or worse. Proceed with extreme caution. The forecast even calls for a 60% chance of flurries tonight, setting up fresh hazards for tomorrow.
</AlertBox>
