### **Watch Out for Western Beltway Construction!**
Listen up, runners, cyclists, and anyone trying to navigate the west side: Clark County is doing work on the 215 Western Beltway Trail. Starting *tomorrow*, Friday, February 27th, and running through the weekend, you'll see closures on the 215 southbound off-ramp to Charleston and later the Charleston on-ramp to 215 southbound. They're installing tunnels for trail users, which is great long-term, but a pain in the short-term. Expect detours and congestion if you're driving or trying to access trails in that area. Also, the Summerlin Parkway Trail project has already shut down Crestdale Lane (between Covington Cross and Trailwood drives) for three months for a tunnel construction. If your usual run involves crossing or utilizing these sections, find an alternate route. Don't be surprised when you hit a dead end and waste perfectly good running time.
</AlertBox>
