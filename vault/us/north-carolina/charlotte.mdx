### On the Trails & Streets: Embrace the Grind (and the Detours)

You want to know about hazards? Charlotte *is* a hazard, but not in the way you might think today. We're talking about the permanent, ever-present hazard of growth. Our city is booming, folks. Uptown, South End, Center City – they're all massive construction zones. We're talking billions in new projects, from apartments to office towers, all either under construction or breaking ground this year. This isn't a new development, it's just *more* of it.

So, if you're planning a run along the Rail Trail in South End, or anywhere near the urban core, expect to dodge some cones, navigate temporary fencing, and probably breathe in a bit more dust than you'd like. The city's open data portal regularly lists closures, and while nothing major has popped up in the last 24 hours to shut down entire running routes for special events, the general construction grind is definitely impacting sidewalks and traffic flow. You might find a detour on your usual path, so stay alert.
