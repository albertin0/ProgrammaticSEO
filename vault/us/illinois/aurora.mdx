## Today's Trail Talk: The Fox River

If you're hitting the **Fox River Trail** today, be mindful. It's an exposed path in many spots, and those crosswinds coming off the water could be a real nuisance, especially if you're trying to hold a pace or ride your bike without getting blown into the river. Keep an eye out for any lingering ice patches in shady areas, particularly under bridges or where the path might not get direct sunlight. "Clear" skies don't magically melt everything overnight.

### Air Quality & Your Lungs

That "Moderate" AQI from ozone is worth paying attention to. Ozone forms when pollutants react in sunlight, and even on cooler, clear days, it can be an issue. It can irritate your respiratory system, causing coughing, throat irritation, and even chest pain. For most of us, it's a minor annoyance, but if you've got asthma or other lung conditions, consider scaling back your intensity or duration. This isn't the day to try and PR your mileage.
