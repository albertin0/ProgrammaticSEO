## The Air: Clear Skies, Not-So-Clear Lungs

Yeah, the sun's out and it's not scorching, which is a win, but don't just breathe easy. Our Air Quality Index is currently sitting at a moderate 61, with Ozone being the dominant pollutant. Mix that with the high fire danger across Central Florida and the breezy conditions, and you've got a recipe for potential respiratory irritation. Those fine particles from brush fires, even far away, can mess with your airways. If you have asthma or any other lung conditions, seriously, consider an indoor workout today. It's not worth pushing it.

## Trail Talk: Urban Trail Still Out of Commission (Partially)

If you're planning a run or ride on the **Orlando Urban Trail**, remember that a significant chunk of it is still closed. The section between Magnolia Avenue and Highland Avenue, near Ivanhoe Village, is shut down for essential Lift Station 2 Force Main Project upgrades. This closure is expected to last a while, so plan your routes accordingly and don't expect a seamless ride through that area. Look for detour signs, but let's be real, they're not always perfectly clear. Be patient and add a few extra miles if you're trying to navigate around it. Also, Fern Creek Avenue at Oregon Street is closed until March 13th for a sanitary project, so if that's part of your usual loop, find an alternate.
