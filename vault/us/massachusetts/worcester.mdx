### The Chill Factor

<AlertBox type="warning">
That "feels like" temperature isn't just a number; it's your body losing heat faster. Exposed skin can get frostbitten in less time than you think, especially if you're not layered correctly. Think balaclavas, insulated gloves, and warm socks. Seriously, you want to come back with all your digits.
</AlertBox>
