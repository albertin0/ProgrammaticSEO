## Today's Hampton Run-Down: Moderate Air, Mild Temps

So, today, February 26th, is shaping up to be one of those "mostly good, but watch your six" kind of days for outdoor workouts in Hampton. The big headline is the air quality. We're sitting at an AQI of 77, with ozone being our dominant pollutant. For most of us, that's fine – you'll breathe normally, maybe feel a slight tickle if you're pushing a tempo run. But if you're asthmatic, have other respiratory issues, or are just particularly sensitive to air pollution, this isn't the day to try for a PR on your usual Grandview Nature Preserve route. You might experience some minor irritation, so listen to your body and don't push it too hard.

On the bright side, the weather itself is quite pleasant. With temperatures around 10.4°C (a comfortable 50.7°F) and clear skies, it's a solid improvement from the dreary, cold mornings we've had. And rejoice, my fellow sufferers, because pollen levels are low. You can actually run outside without feeling like your face is trying to escape your skull.
