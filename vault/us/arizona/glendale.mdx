<AlertBox type="warning">
**Watch Your Step! Construction is Everywhere.**
The City of Glendale is really going for it with the "Central Core Sidewalk Infill Project" and general pavement improvements. This means detours, uneven surfaces, and potential closures, especially around the downtown area and various neighborhood streets like 61st Ave & Townley. Keep your head up and your eyes peeled. Don't be that person who twists an ankle because you were glued to your podcast.
</AlertBox>

<AlertBox type="info">
**AQI is Moderate – Listen to Your Lungs.**
An AQI of 68 with PM10 as the dominant pollutant means it's generally okay for most, but if you're prone to respiratory issues, you might feel it during prolonged or heavy exertion. Consider sticking to routes with less vehicle traffic if possible, especially on days like this where fine particulates are floating around.
</AlertBox>

<AlertBox type="info">
**Hydrate, Even When It's "Cool."**
Yeah, 22.2°C isn't exactly peak summer, but this is Arizona. The air is dry, and you're still sweating. Don't be a hero, carry water. Or at least plan your route past a Circle K.
