## The Air: Breathe Smart, Not Hard

That AQI of 81? It's not "stay indoors and panic" territory, but it's not "gulp down lungfuls of fresh air" either. Fine particulate matter is the dominant pollutant, which is exactly the stuff that gets deep into your lungs and causes issues, especially if you're working hard.

<AlertBox type="warning">
**AQI is Moderate:** For most of you, a moderate AQI means you *can* still work out, but maybe dial back the intensity. If you're asthmatic, have other respiratory issues, or just feel generally cruddy, consider an indoor alternative or a very easy walk. Listen to your body, not your Strava segments. Don't be a hero.
</AlertBox>

## Trail Talk: Guadalupe River Trail

If you were planning a long run or ride along the Guadalupe River Trail today, you need to adjust. The **east trail, south of Tasman Drive**, is officially closed from 7:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. today for levee repairs. They're compacting those levees to fix rodent damage. Yes, really. You'll be detouring, or just finding another spot entirely. The west levee/maintenance road closure starts next week, so at least you've got that side (for now). Don't be "that runner" who ducks under the tape; heavy machinery isn't looking out for your PR.
