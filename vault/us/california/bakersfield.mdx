## Trail Trouble & City Scrapes

Here's the kicker: If you're planning to hit the Kern River Multi-Use Trail, be aware that a segment is closed for construction. Specifically, the Stockdale Ranch extension, about a mile north of the main bike path, is out of commission from February 23rd through April 19th. Don't be surprised when you hit the cones; plan an alternate route or just stick to a different section of the trail. Nothing worse than getting into your rhythm only to hit a "trail closed" sign.

Beyond the specific trail closure, keep your eyes open for general urban hazards. While there are no current widespread flooding warnings for today (we got spared from that atmospheric river, thankfully), construction is always a thing in Bakersfield.
