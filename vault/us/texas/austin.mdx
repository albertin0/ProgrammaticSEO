### **Red Flag Warning for Fire Danger!**
A Red Flag Warning is in effect for the Hill Country surrounding Austin from 11 AM to 7 PM today due to breezy winds (10-25 mph) and extremely low humidity. While central Austin isn't directly under the warning, near-critical fire conditions are expected across all of South Central Texas. This means increased risk of grass fires and potentially some smoke in the air, especially with those winds. Keep an eye out and definitely avoid any activities that could spark a blaze.
</AlertBox>

## Trail & Road Hazards

If your preferred workout involves the Ann and Roy Butler Hike and Bike Trail around Lady Bird Lake, be prepared for some serious navigational challenges. The I-35 Capital Express Central Project is still in full swing, meaning significant closures and detours. The pedestrian ramp to the Boardwalk at Riverside Drive is gone until 2029, and the east-side I-35 pedestrian crossing is closed for roughly a year as they build the new Lady Bird Lake Bridge. You'll need to use the South Congress bridge or the shiny new Wishbone Pedestrian Bridge at Pleasant Valley to cross the lake on the east side.

Expect reroutes on the north shore of Lady Bird Lake through 2033. And if you're venturing further south, the South Lamar corridor project has ongoing work, with the Mary Street roundabout expected to wrap up mid-March.
