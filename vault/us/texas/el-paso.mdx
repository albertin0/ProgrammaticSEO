### Why today is/isn't the day:

If you're looking for an outdoor workout, today's not terrible, but it's not perfect either. The air quality has that lurking "dust devil" potential, and nobody wants a lungful of El Paso grit after a hard mile. So, those of you with sensitive respiratory systems, pay close attention to how you're feeling and maybe keep that workout indoors or choose a less intense activity if the air starts feeling thick.
