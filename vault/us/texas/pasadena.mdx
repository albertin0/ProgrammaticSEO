While not scorching, 26°C with an 83°F heat index is enough to make you sweat buckets if you're not careful. We're looking at "near-record warmth" in the region. If you're planning a run on the Armand Bayou Hike & Bike Trails, especially the more exposed sections, factor in that heat. Go earlier rather than later, carry more water than you think you need, and don't be a hero. Heat exhaustion is a real buzzkill.
