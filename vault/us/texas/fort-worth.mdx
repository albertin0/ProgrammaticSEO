Seriously, Fort Worth, get your act together. The pollen levels are off the charts, and those 20 mph wind gusts aren't doing anyone any favors. If you *must* run, consider an N95 mask to filter out some of the airborne misery, or stick to indoor alternatives. Your lungs will thank you.

### Hydrate, Hydrate, Hydrate!
Even if you're just walking, the warmer, breezy conditions can lead to quicker dehydration than you'd expect. Keep that water bottle handy, folks. It's not July yet, but your body doesn't know the difference when it's dry and windy.
</AlertBox>

Before you head out, especially if you're cycling or driving to a trailhead, glance at the City of Fort Worth's road closures map. Projects are ongoing across town, from Deen Road to North Commerce Street, so your usual path might be blocked or require a creative detour.
